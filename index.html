CHAINSAW-PEDIA

The main characters from the hit manga, Chainsaw Man.

Character Information

Denji
He's a chainsaw. He's a boy. He's Chainsaw Boy, I mean, Man. He's Chainsaw Man. Raaaaaaaaaaaah.
Denji is the protagonist of this series, and the plot follows his story. His pet Chainsaw Devil, Pochita, lives inside his chest as his heart, granting him the power to transform into Chainsaw Man. The day he was able to utilize this power, he was taken in by Public Safety and has been working for them ever since.

Power
She's the Blood Devil!! She's aspiring to become the president of the world!!! She's called Power!!!!
Power is an eccentric devil who is also a pathological liar. She runs about in the dead body of a young woman, making her a Fiend. She was assigned by her boss to work with Denji as buddies in their job.

Aki Hayakawa
He wants to get revenge for his family.. but he's self aware enough to know that he can't get it done alone.
Aki is a serious, but kind man. He's Denji's senior by three years, and was assigned to watch over him as he got used to Public Safety's devil-hunting work. His family, among millions of others, was massacred by the Gun Devil back when he was just a child. He formed a contract with the Fox Devil to help make his work easier.

Makima
A mysterious woman.. Always smiling and always staring. It's hard to tell what she's thinking.
Makima is the woman in charge of the Tokyo Division of Public Safety. Her actions and motivations are always ambigious, but for some reason she has some interest in that kid, Denji.

Asa Mitaka
She died.. and she's still dead.. but she's still living?
Asa is another protagonist in this series, but only shows up in the second part of this story. One night, she gets killed by a classmate who had made a contract with a devil. By some mysterious fate, another devil, Yoru, was present, and offered to keep Asa alive in exchange for letting Yoru possess her body to fight.
